# Sales Revenue Analytics Dashboard

An interactive 2-page business intelligence dashboard built in Power BI, analysing 4 years of retail sales data (2014–2017) across 4 regions and 17 product sub-categories.

## Dashboard Pages

**Page 1 — Sales Overview**
- KPI cards: $2.3M total revenue, $286K profit, 12.5% margin, 5,009 orders
- Revenue trend by year (2014–2017)
- Sales breakdown by region and product category
- Interactive slicers for year and region filtering

**Page 2 — Profitability Analysis**
- Profit by sub-category — identifying loss-making products
- Category-level profit distribution
- Discount vs Profit scatter analysis

## Key Business Insights

- **West region** leads with $725K in sales (31.6% of total revenue)
- **Technology** is the most profitable category at $145K profit (50.8% of total)
- **Tables and Bookcases** generate negative profit (-$17,725 and -$3,472) despite significant sales volume — driven by excessive discount rates
- Overall profit margin of **12.5%** with clear room for improvement in the Furniture category

## Tools & Skills Used

- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data cleaning & transformation with Python (Pandas)
- Data modelling and relationship building
- Business insight generation

## Dataset

Superstore Sales Dataset — 9,994 rows of retail transactional data  
Source: [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)
